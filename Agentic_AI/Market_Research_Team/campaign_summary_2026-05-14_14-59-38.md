## 📊 Refined Trend Insights
Executive Summary: Summer 2026 Sunglasses Campaign

Overview  
Our summer 2026 assortment centers on three dominant eyewear trends—rectangular silhouettes, oversized proportions and performance-driven shapes. By leading with two hero styles (Wayfarer and Sport) and accenting with Mystique, we deliver a cohesive, on-trend story that resonates with both fashion-forward and active consumers.

1. Key Market Trends  
• Refined Rectangular Frames  
  – Neutral palettes, glossy black and tortoise acetate in clean, angular cuts.  
• Subtly Oversized Silhouettes  
  – Bold lens shapes with moderated scale for modern appeal.  
• Sport-Inspired Performance Designs  
  – Wraparound and flat-top profiles in bright acetate with rubber grips and ergonomic details.

2. Hero and Accent Styles  
• Wayfarer (SG002) – Hero  
  – Thick acetate, sharp rectangular lenses; available in tortoise, burgundy or black. Embodies the move toward bold, editorial-friendly shapes.  
• Sport (SG004) – Hero  
  – One-piece wraparound lens, lightweight frame and integrated rubber grips. Fulfills demand for stylish, functional eyewear.  
• Mystique (SG003) – Accent  
  – Soft cat-eye in tortoise pattern with delicate temple details. Provides a refined, vintage-inspired contrast.

3. Campaign Rationale  
• Broad Trend Coverage: Wayfarer anchors the rectangular trend, Sport captures athletic functionality and Mystique introduces a fashionable retro note.  
• Market Appeal: Targets both style-seeking and active consumers with clear, complementary offerings.  
• Merchandising Simplicity: Two headliners streamline messaging; one accent adds depth and choice.

Recommendation  
Position Wayfarer and Sport as primary campaign pillars, supported by Mystique as a premium, fashion-forward option. This approach maximizes relevance across summer’s most influential eyewear trends, driving both brand prestige and sales growth.
